Design a mobile app interface for a 12-year-old boy focused on building healthy habits through sports, fun challenges, and daily coaching.



The user is a sporty boy who enjoys activity but is currently overweight, so the interface must be encouraging, positive, and non-judgmental. The design should feel like a game or sports training companion rather than a health or weight-loss app.





Target User





12-year-old boy
Likes sports, competition, and games
Responds well to progress tracking, challenges, and rewards
Needs motivation to maintain healthy routines around food and activity






Design Style





Bright, energetic colours (sports/game inspired)
Friendly cartoon or illustrated style
Gamified elements (badges, levels, progress bars)
Simple navigation suitable for children
Large buttons and clear icons






Main Screens to Create





1. Personal Dashboard



Greeting: “Hey [Name]! Ready for today’s challenge?”
Daily progress bar
Step/activity tracker
Streak counter
Avatar or character that levels up




2. Daily Missions Screen



3–5 small daily missions such as:
“10 minute football skills”
“Drink water instead of soda”
“Try one new healthy snack”

Missions displayed like game quests
Points awarded for completion




3. Activity Challenge Screen



Sports-style challenges
Weekly leaderboard or progress meter
Examples:
Dribble challenge
Jump challenge
Step goal

Rewards and badges




4. Food Explorer



Kid-friendly healthy food suggestions
Visual snack swaps (e.g., crisps → popcorn)
Simple graphics rather than calorie numbers
“Fuel for your game” theme




5. Rewards & Achievements



Unlockable badges
Points system
Progress levels (Rookie → Pro Athlete → Champion)
Visual trophy cabinet




6. Encouragement / Coaching Messages

Short motivational messages like:



“Great job staying active today!”
“Champions fuel their bodies well.”
“You’re building your strength every day.”






Key Design Principles





Avoid weight or calorie tracking on the child interface
Focus on strength, energy, sports performance and fun
Make it feel like a sports game companion
Clear visual progress to keep motivation high






Output





Create mobile app UI wireframes for iPhone size, including:



Dashboard
Daily missions
Activity challenges
Food explorer
Rewards screen




Style should feel similar to a kids fitness game or sports training app.
